Instant Mug Brownie

Published on December 5, 2025 by The 5-minute Blog

Your emergency chocolate fix, ready in minutes and guaranteed to satisfy even the most dramatic dessert cravings!

Ingredients

Dry ingredients:
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1/8 tsp baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
Wet ingredients:
  • 2 tbsp whole milk
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/4 tsp vanilla extract
Optional: You may add 1 tbsp chocolate chips for extra chocolatey flavour.

Instructions

  1. In a microwave-safe mug (at least 12 oz capacity to prevent overflow), whisk together 1/4 cup all-purpose flour, 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder, 2 tbsp granulated sugar, 1/8 tsp baking powder, and a pinch of salt until no lumps remain.
  2. Add 2 tbsp milk, 1 tbsp vegetable oil, and 1/4 tsp vanilla extract to the dry ingredients.
  3. Stir vigorously with a fork or small whisk until the batter is smooth and fully combined, scraping the sides and bottom of the mug to incorporate all flour.
  4. Gently fold in 1 tbsp chocolate chips if using, distributing them evenly throughout the batter.
  5. Microwave on high power for 60 to 70 seconds (start with 60 seconds for a fudgier center or 70 seconds for a cakier texture).
  6. Carefully remove the mug from the microwave (it will be hot) and let it cool for 2 minutes to set.
  7. Enjoy directly from the mug with a spoon.

Rich chocolate flavor and moist crumb to accompany your day! Enjoy as is or with a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
