Avocado Toast with Cherry Tomatoes

Published on December 5, 2025 by The 5-minute Blog

A new twist on the avocado toast, blending Millenial zing with classic Italian bruscetta flavours!

Ingredients

For the toast:
  • 2 slices sourdough bread (or any of your coice)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
For the toppings:
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp cottage cheese
  • 1 tbsp fresh basil, chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 400Â°F.
  2. Brush both sides of the sourdough bread slices with 1 tbsp olive oil.
  3. Place the bread on a baking sheet and bake for 8-10 minutes, until golden and crispy.
  4. While the toast bakes, cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  5. Mash the avocado with a fork until smooth but slightly chunky.
  6. Stir in 1 tbsp lemon juice, 1/4 tsp salt, and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
  7. Cut the cherry tomatoes in halves and set them aside.
  8. Remove the toast from the oven and let it cool for 1 minute.
  9. Spread the mashed avocado evenly over the warm toast.
  10. Top with the halved cherry tomatoes.
  11. Sprinkle 2 tbsp cottage cheese over the tomatoes.
  12. Garnish with 1 tbsp chopped fresh basil.

The perfect snack to bring on the Mediterranean vibes. If you prefer it more authentic, you can sprinkle crumbled feta cheese or if you'd like a bigger meal you can serve it with a poached egg on top.
