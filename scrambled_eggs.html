Microwave Scrambled Eggs

Published on December 5, 2025 by The 5-minute Blog

Don't look down upon your microwave as a cooking device! This recipe proves that you can save on time without compromising taste.

Ingredients

For the eggs:
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons whole milk
  • ¼ teaspoon kosher salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon black pepper
For finishing:
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Crack 2 large eggs into a microwave-safe bowl.
  2. Add 2 tablespoons whole milk, ¼ teaspoon kosher salt, and ⅛ teaspoon black pepper to the bowl.
  3. Whisk vigorously with a fork for 30 seconds until fully combined and slightly frothy (this incorporates air for fluffier eggs).
  4. Place the bowl in the microwave and cook on high power for 45 seconds.
  5. Remove the bowl using oven mitts (it will be hot!) and stir the eggs with a fork, scraping the cooked edges toward the center.
  6. Return the bowl to the microwave and cook for another 30-45 seconds until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly moist.
  7. Add 1 tablespoon unsalted butter to the hot eggs and let it melt for 15 seconds without stirring.
  8. Gently fold the melted butter into the eggs until just incorporated (overmixing can make them rubbery).
  9. Let the eggs rest for 1 minute to finish cooking from residual heat.

This is a low calorie choice that still packs the protein for the beginning of your day. For extra options you can pile on toast or stuff into breakfast tacos.
