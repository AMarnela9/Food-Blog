Banana-Peanut Butter Smoothie

Published on December 5, 2025 by The 5-minute Blog

A banana peanut butter smoothie is the perfect drink for those that want both a ridiculously healthy and decadently delicious choice!

Ingredients

For the smoothie base:
  • 2 large ripe bananas, peeled and frozen
  • 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup ice cubes
For optional toppings (choose one or mix):
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 2 tbsp granola
  • 1/4 cup sliced strawberries

Instructions

  1. Add 2 large frozen bananas, 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter, 1 cup unsweetened almond milk, 1 tbsp honey, and 1/2 tsp vanilla extract to a high-speed blender. Pro tip: Freeze your bananas when they’re spotty-ripe for maximum natural sweetness and creamier texture—no sugar needed!
  2. Blend on high speed for 45 seconds until the mixture is smooth and no banana chunks remain.
  3. Add 1 cup ice cubes to the blender.
  4. Blend again on high for another 30 seconds until the smoothie is thick, frosty, and fully homogeneous. Pro tip: If your blender struggles, pause and scrape down the sides with a spatula to ensure everything incorporates evenly.
  5. Pour immediately into two tall glasses, dividing equally. Pro tip: For an extra protein boost, sprinkle 1 tbsp chia seeds over the top and let them sit for 2 minutes to form a fun gel-like layer.
  6. Gently fold the melted butter into the eggs until just incorporated (overmixing can make them rubbery).
  7. Garnish with 2 tbsp granola or 1/4 cup sliced strawberries if desired for crunch and color.

This can be the creamiest start to your day or a much needed post workout refresher! Perfect balance of sugar, fiber and protein for those on the run looking to skip a meal. Choose your own non-dairy choice and supplement honey accordingly.
